# Love Takes Authenticity

> _Meaningful connections start where values align._

---

## The Problem

Modern dating apps are broken — not by accident, but by design.

They are engineered to keep you swiping, not to help you find someone. They monetize loneliness, gate basic features behind paywalls, and optimize for engagement over genuine connection. The result is a cycle of superficial matches, shallow interactions, and emotional exhaustion.

We believe the industry has gotten it backwards. Love is one of the most profound forces in human life. It makes us better people. It deserves better software.

## The Idea

Lasting relationships are not built on aesthetics or proximity. They are built on **shared values**.

When two people fundamentally care about the same things — honesty, growth, empathy, responsibility — they have a foundation that can weather almost anything. When they don't, even the most exciting chemistry eventually fractures along those fault lines.

**Love Takes Authenticity** is a dating platform centered around that insight. Before you ever see a profile, you clarify who you are — your core personal values. The platform then uses that self-knowledge to surface people who are genuinely compatible with you, not just algorithmically convenient.

No dark patterns. No artificial scarcity. No exploiting loneliness for profit.

## Features

| Feature                   | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Values-Based Matching** | Users are matched primarily on shared core personal values, not just interests or aesthetics.              |
| **Values Finder**         | An interactive assistant that helps users who are unsure of their own values discover and articulate them. |
| **Rich User Profiles**    | Profiles that go beyond photos — space for context, personality, and what you are actually looking for.    |
| **Conversations**         | A clean, distraction-free messaging space for matched users to connect.                                    |

## Philosophy

- **Authenticity over performance.** We encourage users to show up as they are, not as who they think others want to see.
- **Values are the foundation.** Shared deep values predict long-term compatibility better than shared hobbies or physical attraction alone.
- **People over profit.** This is an open-source project. There are no investors to appease and no growth metrics that come at the user's expense.
- **Respect by default.** The platform is designed to foster genuine human connection, not addiction.

## Tech Stack

The project is a monorepo with three workspaces:
